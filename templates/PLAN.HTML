NeoArcana Business Plan 2025

Fusion of Traditional Tarot Art and Modern Technology

Executive Summary

NeoArcana represents an innovative fusion of traditional tarot art and cutting-edge technology, offering limited edition NFC-enabled tarot art prints that connect to personalized AI readings. Created by a student artist in Austria, the project combines AI-enhanced artwork with human artistic refinement and modern technology.

Product Overview

Core Features

  • Limited edition tarot art prints (46x61 cm)
  • NFC-enabled for interactive digital experience
  • AI-powered personalized readings
  • Multi-language support (9 languages)
  • Custom card reveal animations
  • Progressive Web App functionality

Technical Infrastructure

Web Application

  • Custom Flask backend
  • React frontend components
  • Cloud hosting
  • CI/CD pipeline

Content Creation Tools

  • Card reveal animation system
  • Green screen video generator
  • Social media content automation
